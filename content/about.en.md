## About This Blog

This blog documents practical engineering work across hardware and software. The focus is on lessons that come from real implementation rather than abstract summaries.

Main topics include:

- PCB design and embedded systems
- Software development and backend/frontend engineering
- AI, automation, and agent workflows
- DevOps, tooling, and engineering productivity

The goal is to publish material that is directly useful to engineers who build, debug, and operate real systems.
